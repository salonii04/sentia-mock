# Sentia AI 🌱

Welcome to Sentia AI, your personal sanctuary for mental wellness and emotional growth. 

Sentia AI is designed to be a calming, interactive companion that helps you navigate your feelings, celebrate your wins, and cultivate mindfulness through the beautiful metaphor of a growing garden.

## 🌿 Your Mind Garden

At the heart of Sentia AI is your **Mind Garden**. As you interact with the app, you earn seeds that you can use to buy and plant beautiful flowers. Your garden is a living reflection of your journey.

*   **Plant with Intention:** Choose from a variety of flowers like Roses, Sunflowers, Tulips, and Jasmine. Place them anywhere in your garden to create a unique, personalized landscape.
*   **A Space to Breathe:** The serene environment, complete with a gentle stone path and a rustic fountain, provides a safe space to pause and reflect.
*   **Watch it Grow:** Every flower you plant is a testament to the time you've dedicated to your well-being.

## 🐧 Meet Pip, Your Companion

You're never alone in your garden. Pip, your empathetic penguin companion, is always there to listen.

*   **Share Your Heart:** Whether you're feeling overwhelmed by a setback or overflowing with joy from a recent success, Pip understands.
*   **Guided Conversations:** Pip responds thoughtfully to your emotions, offering comforting words when you're down and celebrating with you when you're up.
*   **A Dynamic Environment:** The world around you responds to your conversations. A difficult day might bring a gentle, reflective rain to the garden, while sharing good news might make a vibrant rainbow appear. 

## 📖 Reflect and Grow

Sentia AI also helps you track your emotional journey over time.

*   **Mood Calendar:** Your daily interactions gently color your calendar in soothing shades of green, giving you a visual map of your month.
*   **Personal Diary:** Revisit your past conversations and reflections. Important moments are saved as diary entries, allowing you to look back on how you felt and how you navigated those feelings with Pip's help.
*   **Build Healthy Habits:** Keep your daily streak alive and watch your seed count grow as you make checking in with yourself a regular part of your routine.
